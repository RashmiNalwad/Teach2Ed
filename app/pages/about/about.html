Teach2Educate App

Version 1.0

Released on 2016

Mission: To build very strong learning of concept during the under grad education and helps to bring in the self confidence with each individual to learn the concept and become confident in teaching the same to peers

Teach2Educate is non profit organisation, team of student from the US university wanted to build the app in order to help the under graduate to firmly understand the concept during the academic year
